<p><p>The micro irrigation system market includes various types that cater to efficient water distribution in agriculture. Drip irrigation delivers precise water directly to plant roots, minimizing evaporation. Micro/Mini sprinkler irrigation uses smaller sprinklers for targeted coverage, while conventional sprinkler irrigation applies water over larger areas. Traditional sprinklers are manually operated for flexibility. Central pivot sprinklers rotate around a pivot point for uniform coverage across vast fields, and lateral move sprinklers traverse fields in a lateral motion, ensuring efficient irrigation practices.</p></p>
