<p><p>The hip and knee implants market is segmented into three main types. Primary implants are used for first-time joint replacements, providing full support and mobility restoration. Partial or unicompartmental implants target only damaged sections of the joint, preserving healthy tissue and allowing for a less invasive procedure. Revision implants are utilized in replacement surgeries following failed primary implants, addressing complications such as wear or loosening, and ensuring improved joint function and longevity for patients.</p></p>
