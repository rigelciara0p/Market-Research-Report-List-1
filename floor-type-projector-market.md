<p><ul><li>Digital Light Processing (DLP)</li><li>Liquid Crystal Display (LCD)</li><li>Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floor type projector market comprises three primary technologies: Digital Light Processing (DLP), Liquid Crystal Display (LCD), and Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS). DLP projectors use micro-mirrors for high-quality images and are known for their compact size and reliability. LCD projectors utilize liquid crystal panels for vivid colors and clarity, making them popular for presentations. LCoS technology combines elements of both DLP and LCD, offering superior resolution and color accuracy, ideal for high-definition applications and detailed visual displays.</p></p>
