<p><p>Ball bearings are mechanical components that facilitate smooth rotational or linear movement by reducing friction between moving parts. Typically made from steel, ceramic, or plastic, they consist of spherical balls housed between inner and outer rings. These bearings are commonly used in various applications, including automotive, aerospace, machinery, and consumer electronics.</p><p>The ball bearing market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for efficient machinery across industries.
