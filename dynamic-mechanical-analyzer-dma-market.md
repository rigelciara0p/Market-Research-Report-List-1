<p><p>Dynamic Mechanical Analyzers (DMA) are instruments used to measure the mechanical properties of materials as a function of temperature, time, and frequency. The market for DMA is segmented into two main types: Forced Resonance Analyzers and Free Resonance Analyzers. Forced Resonance Analyzers apply a consistent oscillatory force to the sample, enabling precise control over frequency and amplitude. In contrast, Free Resonance Analyzers allow the sample to resonate freely, capturing its natural vibrational response, leading to insights into material behavior and characteristics.</p></p>
