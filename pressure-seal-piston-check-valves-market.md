<p><p>Pressure seal piston check valves are designed to prevent backflow in piping systems while maintaining high-pressure integrity. The market offers various types, including socket weld and butt welding types. Socket weld valves are attached using a socket and fillet weld, facilitating easy installation in smaller pipes. Conversely, butt welding valves are joined directly at the pipe ends, offering a stronger connection ideal for high-pressure applications. Both types are prevalent in industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and chemicals.</p></p>
