<p><p>SaaS-based Supply Chain Management (SCM) software can be categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise SCM solutions are installed locally on a company's hardware, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based SCM software is hosted on remote servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and reduced IT overhead, as updates and maintenance are managed by the service provider. Overall, cloud solutions are increasingly preferred for their accessibility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
