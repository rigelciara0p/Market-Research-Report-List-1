<p><p>The Digital Telemetry System market encompasses two primary types: Single Channel Telemetry and Multi-Channel Telemetry. Single Channel Telemetry focuses on transmitting data from one sensor or source, making it ideal for simple applications requiring real-time monitoring. In contrast, Multi-Channel Telemetry supports multiple sensors, enabling simultaneous data collection and monitoring from various sources. This versatility allows for comprehensive data analysis in complex environments, such as industrial processes, healthcare, and environmental monitoring, thereby enhancing operational efficiency and decision-making.</p></p>
