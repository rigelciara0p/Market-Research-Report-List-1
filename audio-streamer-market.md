<p><p>The audio streamer market is primarily divided into two main types: Bluetooth streamers and Wi-Fi streamers. Bluetooth streamers focus on short-range connectivity, offering convenience and portability for users to connect devices like smartphones and tablets to speakers or headphones. In contrast, Wi-Fi streamers enable high-quality, longer-range audio transmission, often incorporating multi-room capabilities and supporting various streaming services. Both types cater to differing user needs, with Bluetooth ideal for mobility and Wi-Fi suited for home audio systems.</p></p>
