<p><p>The HVDC PP film capacitors market is segmented into two main types based on converter technology: Voltage Source Converters (VSC) and Line Commutated Converters (LCC). VSCs allow for greater control over power flow and are suitable for applications requiring flexible energy management, such as connecting renewable energy sources. In contrast, LCCs rely on the existing AC grid for commutation, making them ideal for high-capacity, long-distance transmission. Both technologies leverage HVDC PP film capacitors for improved reliability and efficiency in power transmission systems.</p></p>
