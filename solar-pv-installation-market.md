<p><strong>The Solar PV Installation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Off-grid PV System</li><li>Grid-connected PV System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar PV installation market comprises two primary types: off-grid and grid-connected PV systems. Off-grid PV systems operate independently from the electricity grid, providing power to remote areas or locations without grid access, often supplemented by battery storage. In contrast, grid-connected PV systems are linked to the utility grid, allowing for net metering and the sale of excess energy back to the grid, enhancing energy security and offsetting utility costs for residential and commercial users.</p></p>
