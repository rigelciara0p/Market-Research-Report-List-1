<p><p>The Point-type Flame Detector market primarily comprises two types: Infrared (IR) and Ultraviolet (UV) detectors. Infrared detectors identify flame presence by sensing the heat emitted in the infrared spectrum, making them effective for various fuel types. In contrast, Ultraviolet detectors detect the specific UV radiation produced by flames, offering rapid response times and high sensitivity to fires. Both types serve critical roles in industrial safety, providing early warning systems to prevent catastrophic events in environments prone to flammable materials.</p></p>
