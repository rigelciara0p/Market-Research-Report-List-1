<p><strong>The Digital Mammography Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Digital Mammography (FFDM)</li><li>Digital Breast Tomography (DBT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital mammography machine market primarily comprises two types: Full Digital Mammography (FFDM) and Digital Breast Tomography (DBT). FFDM utilizes digital sensors to capture high-resolution images of breast tissue, enhancing detection capabilities and patient comfort. In contrast, DBT, often referred to as 3D mammography, takes multiple X-ray images of the breast from different angles, creating a layered view that improves the visualization of tumors while minimizing false positives. Both technologies aim to improve breast cancer screening outcomes.</p></p>
