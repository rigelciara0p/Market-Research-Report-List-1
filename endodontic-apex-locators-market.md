<p><ul><li>Electronic Apex Locators</li><li>Traditional Apex Locators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endodontic apex locators market comprises two primary types: electronic apex locators and traditional apex locators. Electronic apex locators utilize advanced technologies, such as impedance or frequency-based methods, to accurately determine the root canal's apical foramen position, enhancing treatment precision. In contrast, traditional apex locators typically rely on subjective visual indicators, such as radiographs or tactile feedback, which may be less accurate. The shift toward electronic devices reflects a growing preference for efficiency and reliability in endodontic procedures.</p></p>
