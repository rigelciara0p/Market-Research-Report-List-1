<p><p>The pain management devices market encompasses various technologies aimed at alleviating pain. Neuromodulation devices, such as spinal cord stimulators, adjust nerve activity to reduce pain perception. Ablation devices utilize heat or cold to destroy pain-inducing tissues. Analgesic infusion pumps deliver medication directly to the pain site, providing targeted relief. Additionally, other devices include neurostimulators and transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulation (TENS) units, which offer non-invasive alternatives for managing pain. Collectively, these devices enhance patient quality of life through effective pain control.</p></p>
