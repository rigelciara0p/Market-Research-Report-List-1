<p><ul><li>Pressure Vacuum Breaker (PVB)</li><li>Reduced Pressure Zone (RPZ)</li><li>Double Check Assembly (DCA)</li><li>Atmospheric Vacuum Breaker (AVB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The backflow prevention market comprises various types designed to stop contaminated water from reversing into clean water systems. The Pressure Vacuum Breaker (PVB) is ideal for non-continuous applications, while the Reduced Pressure Zone (RPZ) provides enhanced protection for high-risk scenarios. The Double Check Assembly (DCA) offers reliable backflow prevention in low-risk situations, whereas the Atmospheric Vacuum Breaker (AVB) is suitable for simple applications. Each type serves unique protection needs, driving demand across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
