<p><p>The air quality sensors market consists of two main types: signal-gas monitoring sensors and multi-gas monitoring sensors. Signal-gas monitoring sensors are designed to detect specific pollutants, providing focused data on targeted gases such as carbon monoxide or ozone. In contrast, multi-gas monitoring sensors can measure multiple pollutants simultaneously, offering a comprehensive overview of air quality. This versatility makes multi-gas sensors valuable for applications requiring broader environmental assessments, while signal-gas sensors are ideal for precise pollution tracking.</p></p>
