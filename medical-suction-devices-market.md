<p><p>The medical suction devices market can be categorized into two main types: consumables and equipment. Consumables refer to disposable items such as suction catheters, canisters, and tubing that are used temporarily and require regular replacement to maintain hygiene and efficacy. Equipment encompasses the more permanent devices, such as portable or stationary suction pumps and vacuum systems, which facilitate the removal of fluids and debris from patients. Both segments are essential for effective medical procedures and patient care.</p></p>
