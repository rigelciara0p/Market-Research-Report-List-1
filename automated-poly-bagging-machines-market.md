<p><p>Automated poly bagging machines are categorized into two main types: horizontal and vertical bagging machines. Horizontal bagging machines primarily work by feeding products from one side and sealing bags horizontally, ideal for bulkier items. In contrast, vertical bagging machines utilize a vertical feed system, making them suitable for smaller, granulated products. Both types enhance efficiency and reduce labor costs in packaging operations, catering to various industries by automating the bagging process and ensuring consistent product presentation.</p></p>
