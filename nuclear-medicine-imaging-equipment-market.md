<p><p>Nuclear medicine imaging equipment includes Full Ring and Partial Ring PET scanners, both used for advanced imaging techniques. Full Ring PET scanners feature a complete circular detector configuration, providing comprehensive coverage and improved image quality for whole-body scans. In contrast, Partial Ring PET scanners have a limited detection area, making them more compact and cost-effective, suitable for specific applications or facilities with space constraints. Both types play essential roles in diagnosing and monitoring various medical conditions through precise imaging.</p></p>
