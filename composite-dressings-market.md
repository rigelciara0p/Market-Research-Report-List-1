<p><strong>The Composite Dressings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alginate</li><li>Absorbent</li><li>Anti-Microbial</li><li>CMC</li><li>Silver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite dressings market includes various types designed for specific wound care needs. Alginate dressings, made from seaweed, are highly absorbent and promote healing. Absorbent dressings manage exudate and maintain a moist environment. Anti-microbial dressings incorporate agents to prevent infection, crucial for surgical and chronic wounds. Carboxymethyl cellulose (CMC) dressings maintain moisture while providing a soothing barrier. Silver dressings leverage the antimicrobial properties of silver to combat infection, particularly in complex or infected wounds. These diverse dressings enhance treatment efficacy and patient outcomes.</p></p>
