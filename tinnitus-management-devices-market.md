<p><p>The tinnitus management devices market includes sound masking devices, notched music devices, and hearing aids. Sound masking devices generate white noise or soothing sounds to conceal tinnitus, providing relief. Notched music devices play music with specific frequencies removed, allowing the brain to adapt and reduce the perception of tinnitus over time. Hearing aids enhance overall hearing capabilities, which can help those with tinnitus by improving communication and reducing the focus on ringing or buzzing sounds. Together, they offer effective management strategies for tinnitus sufferers.</p></p>
