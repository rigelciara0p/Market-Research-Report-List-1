<p><ul><li>Direct ELISA</li><li>Indirect ELISA</li><li>Competition ELISA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The enzyme immunoassay (EIA) reagents and devices market is categorized into three main types: Direct ELISA, Indirect ELISA, and Competitive ELISA. </p><p>Direct ELISA involves the direct attachment of an enzyme-labeled antibody to the target antigen, allowing for quick detection. Indirect ELISA uses a primary antibody to bind the antigen, followed by a secondary enzyme-labeled antibody for amplification, enhancing sensitivity. Competitive ELISA measures the concentration of an antigen by competing with a labeled antigen for binding sites, offering quantitative analysis in various applications, including diagnostics and research.</p></p>
