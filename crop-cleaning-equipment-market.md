<p><p>The crop cleaning equipment market is categorized into two main types: pre-cleaning and fine cleaning. Pre-cleaning equipment focuses on the initial removal of larger impurities, such as stones and debris, from harvested crops. This stage prepares the crops for further processing. Fine cleaning equipment, on the other hand, targets smaller contaminants like dust and husks, ensuring a high-quality end product. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing crop quality and enhancing overall efficiency in the agricultural processing industry.</p></p>
