<p><p>The Offline Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment market is primarily divided into two types: 2D AOI Equipment and 3D AOI Equipment. </p><p>2D AOI Equipment analyzes the surface features of electronic components using two-dimensional imaging, focusing on detecting defects such as misalignments or soldering issues. </p><p>In contrast, 3D AOI Equipment utilizes advanced imaging techniques to create three-dimensional representations, enabling the inspection of height and volume for more accurate detection of defects like voids and bridging. Both types enhance quality control in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
