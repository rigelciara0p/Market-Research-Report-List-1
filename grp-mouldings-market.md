Special wax is utilized as a release agent to prevent adhesion between the mould and the finished product, ensuring smooth surface finishes. PVA serves a similar purpose, offering excellent release properties along with a protective film that enhances surface quality. Both materials contribute significantly to the efficiency of the moulding process and the quality of GRP products across various applications, including automotive and construction.</p></p>
