<p><strong>The Automotive Connected Infotainment System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded Systems</li><li>Smartphone Integrated Systems</li><li>Tethered System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive connected infotainment system market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Embedded Systems** are fully integrated into the vehicle, offering navigation, multimedia, and communication without relying on external devices. </p><p>2. **Smartphone Integrated Systems** enable drivers to connect their smartphones directly to the vehicle’s interface, allowing seamless access to apps and features.</p><p>3. **Tethered Systems** connect the vehicle to a smartphone via a wire, providing basic connectivity while utilizing the phone's capabilities for enhanced functionality. Together, these systems enhance the driving experience through connectivity and convenience.</p></p>
