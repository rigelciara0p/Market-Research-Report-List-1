<p><ul><li>Receiver</li><li>Transmitter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cell phone wireless charger market comprises two main types: receivers and transmitters. Receivers are integrated into smartphones and convert electromagnetic fields into electrical energy, enabling the device to charge wirelessly. Transmitters, on the other hand, are the charging pads or stations that emit electromagnetic fields. Both components work in tandem to facilitate efficient wireless charging, offering convenience and eliminating the need for physical connectors. This market is growing due to increasing demand for seamless and user-friendly charging solutions.</p></p>
