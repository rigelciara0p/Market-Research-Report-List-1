<p><p>The solar panel nano coatings market comprises predominantly two types: hydrophilic and hydrophobic coatings. Hydrophilic coatings attract water, promoting efficient cleaning by allowing dirt and grime to wash away more easily with rain. This enhances light transmission and overall panel efficiency. In contrast, hydrophobic coatings repel water, creating a protective layer that prevents water absorption and dirt accumulation. Both types aim to improve the performance and longevity of solar panels, ultimately contributing to increased energy generation and reduced maintenance costs.</p></p>
