<p><p>Metal Additive Manufacturing Systems encompass various technologies, primarily including Powder Bed Fusion, Directed Energy Deposition, Binder Jetting, and Sheet Lamination. Powder Bed Fusion utilizes lasers to fuse metal powders layer by layer, creating intricate geometries. Directed Energy Deposition involves melting material as it is deposited, allowing repair and additive manufacturing. Binder Jetting applies a binding agent to powder layers, enabling the creation of shapes before sintering. Sheet Lamination joins metal sheets to fabricate parts, offering cost-effective solutions for specific applications.</p></p>
