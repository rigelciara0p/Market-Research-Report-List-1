<p><strong>The Organic Filler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoset</li><li>Termoplastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic filler market is divided into two main types: thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset fillers are polymers that undergo a permanent chemical change when heated, providing strong structural integrity and heat resistance, making them ideal for applications in adhesives and composites. In contrast, thermoplastic fillers can be reshaped upon heating and are versatile for applications requiring flexibility, such as packaging and automotive components. Both types enhance the properties of materials, improving strength, durability, and overall performance.</p></p>
