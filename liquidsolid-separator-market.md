<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Type</li><li>Precipitation Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid/solid separator market includes two primary types: centrifugal separators and precipitation separators. Centrifugal separators utilize high-speed rotation to create a centrifugal force that separates solid particles from liquids efficiently. They are commonly employed in various industries for fast and effective separation. Precipitation separators rely on the natural settling of suspended solids in a liquid over time, which can be enhanced with chemicals to speed up the process. Both types serve critical roles in waste treatment and industrial processing.</p></p>
