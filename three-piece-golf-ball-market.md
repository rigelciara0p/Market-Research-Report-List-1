<p><p>The three-piece golf ball market is primarily divided into two main types: Surlyn and Urethane golf balls. Surlyn golf balls, made from a durable, ionomer resin, are known for their resilience and distance, making them ideal for recreational players. In contrast, Urethane golf balls, crafted from a softer, more premium material, offer better spin control and feel, appealing to advanced golfers seeking enhanced performance around the greens. Both types cater to different player preferences and skill levels, impacting overall market dynamics.</p></p>
