<p><strong>The Substrate Support Mat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intumescent Substrate Support Mat</li><li>Non-Intumescent Substrate Support Mat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The substrate support mat market is divided into two main types: intumescent and non-intumescent substrates. Intumescent substrate support mats expand when exposed to heat, providing additional insulation and protection against fire, making them ideal for high-risk environments. In contrast, non-intumescent substrate support mats do not expand under heat but offer mechanical support and stability in various applications. Both types are essential in construction and industrial settings, ensuring safety and structural integrity for a range of materials.</p></p>
