<p><p>The Connected Medical Technology market encompasses various communication technologies that enable the transmission of health data. Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) facilitates short-range connections for devices like wearables. Wi-Fi supports high-speed data transfer within a local network. Near Field Communication (NFC) allows for quick, close-range interactions, ideal for patient identification. Cellular technology ensures broader coverage for mobile health applications. Satellite communications provide service in remote areas, while ZigBee offers low-power, short-range communication ideal for home healthcare devices. Each technology plays a vital role in enhancing patient care and data accessibility.</p></p>
