<p><p>Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded Pipe (LSAW) is a type of steel pipe primarily used in large-diameter applications, critical for transporting oil, gas, and water. The submerged arc welding method ensures strong, high-quality welds and allows for the production of thick-walled pipes that can withstand high pressures.</p><p>The LSAW pipe market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand from key end-user industries such as oil and gas, construction, and infrastructure development.
