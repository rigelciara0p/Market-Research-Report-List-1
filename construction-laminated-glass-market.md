<p><p>Construction Laminated Glass is a type of safety glass that consists of two or more layers of glass bonded together with an interlayer, typically made of polyvinyl butyral (PVB) or ethylene-vinyl acetate (EVA). This construction not only improves the glass's strength and durability but also enhances sound insulation and UV protection. Due to these properties, laminated glass is increasingly favored in architectural applications and has become essential in modern construction projects.</p><p>The Construction Laminated Glass Market is experiencing significant growth, anticipated to expand at a CAGR of 13.5% during the forecast period. Furthermore, the rising trend of sustainable construction practices drives the adoption of laminated glass, which is recyclable and contributes to energy conservation. <p><p>The construction laminated glass market consists of several types based on interlayer materials. PVB (Polyvinyl Butyral) is widely used for its excellent adhesion, sound insulation, and UV resistance. EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate) offers enhanced transparency and moisture resistance, making it suitable for diverse climates. SGP (SentryGlas Plus) provides superior strength and durability, ideal for high-stress environments. Other market types may include specialized interlayers for specific applications, combining various functionalities such as security, aesthetics, and energy efficiency in building designs.</p></p>
