<p><ul><li>Dynamic</li><li>Static</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical compression pump market can be categorized into static and dynamic types. Static compression pumps provide consistent pressure to control swelling and improve venous return, ideal for post-surgical recovery or chronic venous insufficiency. In contrast, dynamic compression pumps offer varying pressure levels that mimic natural muscle contractions, enhancing blood circulation during treatment for lymphedema or during rehabilitation. Each type serves specific medical needs, catering to different patient conditions and treatment protocols within the compression therapy landscape.</p></p>
